About Us

The Definitive Place For Your Medical Tourism Needs

Safe Health provides its patients with excellent service regarding their required treatment while providing them with robust health tourism services that include services such as carers/guides, visa processing, ticketing, ambulance/air ambulance service and hotel bookings.

As Bangladesh's medical treatment facilities can be lacking and delayed at times, we at Safe Health, do our best to help you locate the best hospitals outside the country at unbeatable rates.

  • We provide around the clock service for our clients and patients
  • We strive to match our patients up with doctors and hospitals that best suit their needs
  • From your initial appointment setting, to your after-surgery care, to all the way up to your return in the country, we will be with you in your Journey

Services

We Provide Complete Services To All Our Patients

Doctor's Appointment

We connect you with trusted specialists for expert advice and the best treatment options. Let us arrange your appointments, ensuring you get the care you need, no matter where you are.

Hospital Admission

Navigating hospital admissions in a new place can be overwhelming. We streamline the process, offering support to make your experience stress-free. Our team and hospital professionals work together to ensure you understand your treatment plan and have a seamless admission.

Visa Processing Assistance

After selecting your treatment hospital, we assist with obtaining the necessary appointment letter for your medical visa. Our staff guides you through the documentation process, ensuring your application is completed accurately and efficiently.

Airline and Bus Ticketing

Booking bus or airline tickets can be challenging with tight schedules and sudden changes. Our staff is here to help, making the process smooth and hassle-free, so you can focus on your treatment.

Road and Air Ambulance

We offer both road and air ambulance services tailored to your needs, ensuring safe and efficient transportation for medical emergencies or transfers.

Hotel Booking

Finding the right hotel can be confusing and stressful. Our team assists you in booking accommodations that fit your budget and specific requirements, providing a comfortable stay during your medical journey.
